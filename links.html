Links

Here's some places on the interwebs that I like

  • tilde.town

    A wonderful community of technology-enjoyers, and where one of the mirrors of Pilosophos' Circle is hosted.

  • Corru Observer

    A cool web game about exploring a fragmented alien biocomputer. No doubt inspired by the Fauux aesthetic.

  • Fauux's ⌈Wired Sound For Wired People⌋

    The famous Lain fansite(?) that feels more like computer art than anything.

  • Raocow's Talkhaus

    The forum of Raocow, the only YouTube let's player who I can still stand even after some 15+ years of LPing. The Talkhaus makes some neat things sometimes, like ASMT.

  • Windows 98 Icon Viewer

    A collection of icons ripped from Windows 98 in png format. Most of the icons on Pilo's Circle come from here.

  • 98.css

    Hands down the best Windows 98 css theme. Bootstrap NOT required.

  • The Ultimate Oldschool PC Font Pack

    A collection of fonts ripped from old DOS-era PCs. Two fonts used on my website, DOS/V re. JPN24 and NEC APC3 8x16, are from here.

  • Moebuntu

    A customization package to moe-nize [sic] your Ubuntu install. もえもえきゅ～ん！
