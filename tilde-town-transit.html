All about tilde.town transit

Tilde.town transit is the public train network on the tilde.town modded Minecraft server.

Lines

1 Line

The 1 Line serves ~pilosophos and ~nebula station across Townshend Bridge. It is a fully single-tracked route and only served by one train at a time. It became operational on the 24th of June, 2024.

Mascot

The 1 Line's mascot is Townshend the Raccoon, who drives the TTT Type I "Carrot" trolley on this route. He was rescued from an oil seep near the house that would eventually become ~pilosophos station.

2 Line

The 2 Line serves Spawn and ~nebula station, the longest route on the network. It is partially double-tracked and shares part of its rail with the upcoming 3 Line.

Mascot

The 2 Line's mascot is Tilly the Chicken, who drives the TTT Type II "Beet" trolley on this route.

3 Line

The 3 Line is a planned line that will serve ~cassii2 and ~nebula station. A mascot has not been selected for the 3 Line yet.

Rolling stock

TTT Type I "Carrot" trolley

The TTT Type I "Carrot" trolley was designed specifically for tilde.town transit and is the network's first train ever deployed. Its design was inspired by the trolleys on the San Francisco cable car system, without the sideways-facing open-air seats.

It is nicknamed the "Carrot" after its orange color, made primarily of Acacia wood planks.

TTT Type II "Beet" trolley

While the Type I "Carrot" was originally deployed on the 2 Line during testing before line opened, the open-air driver cabin of the Carrot's design meant that monsters that spawned in the wilderness at night could attack the driver with arrows during operation. Thus, the Type II "Beet" was created with a fortified steel cabin to keep the driver safe.

Since its maiden voyage in July 2024, the Beet has been retrofit with additional armor and more tightly covered windows.
